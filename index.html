Check-In Volunteer Guide

Step-by-step instructions for the distribution check-in flow

🔍 Before You Start - Quick Checklist

  • Check your phone/device is charged
  • Ensure you have access to the BAM Check-In system
  • Know today's distribution items
0 of 4 completed
1

Landing Page - Today's Appointments

When you open the BAM Check-In system, you'll see Today's Appointments view. This shows all recipients scheduled for today's distribution.

📍 Landing Page: Today's Appointments view with recipients scheduled for distribution
💡 Tip: The "Today's Appointments" view automatically filters to show only recipients with appointments for today's date.
2

Search by Last 4 Digits of Phone Number

When a recipient arrives, use Ctrl+F (or Cmd+F on Mac) and type the last 4 digits of their phone number to find them.

📱 Use Ctrl+F (Cmd+F on Mac) and type the last 4 digits of the recipient's phone number
💡 Tip: Ask the recipient for the last 4 digits of their phone number on file. This is the fastest way to find them.
3

View Recipient's Open Requests

Click on the recipient's name to expand and see their requests. Focus on items with "Open" status - these are the items for the current distribution.

📦 Expanded view showing recipient's requests - focus on "Open" status items
⚠️ Important: Only process items with "Open" status. Items marked "Delivered" have already been given out.
4

Process Each Request

For each Open request, ask the recipient if they still need the item:

If they need the item AND we delivered it → Mark as Delivered:

✅ Click the status dropdown and select "Delivered" when the item has been handed to the recipient

If they don't need the item → Mark as Timeout:

⏱️ Click the status dropdown and select "Timeout" when recipient no longer needs the item

If they need the item but we don't have it → Leave as Open:

⚠️ Important: If the recipient still wants the item but it's not in stock or not approved for purchase yet, leave the request as "Open". The recipient keeps their place in the queue and will be called when the item becomes available. Do NOT mark it as Delivered or Timeout.
💡 Tip: Process ALL open requests for the current distribution before moving to the next recipient. Some requests may stay open across multiple distributions — that's normal.

After all requests are processed → Mark as Checked In:

✅ Once all requests are processed, mark the recipient as "Checked In"
⚡

Early/Late Arrivals

🚨 Scenario: Someone shows up but they're scheduled for a different day (early or late arrival).

Follow these steps to update their appointment to today:

Step A: Go to the Appointments interface (in the left sidebar)
Navigate to Appointments in the sidebar
Step B: Find the person using Ctrl+F (Cmd+F on Mac) to search by the last 4 digits of their phone number, then click on their Appointment Date field
Click on the Appointment Date to open the calendar picker
Step C: Select today's date from the calendar picker
📅 Select today's date to update the appointment
Step D: Go back to BAM Check-In → Today's Appointments - the person should now appear!
✅ After updating, return to Today's Appointments - they should now appear!
Step E: Continue with the normal BAM check-in flow (search, process requests, mark delivered/timeout)
✓

End of Distribution Routine

At the end of each distribution day:

  1. Review that all checked-in recipients have had their requests processed
  2. Verify today's distribution items have been marked as "Delivered" or "Timeout" where applicable. Items that are still "Open" because they weren't in stock stay open — that's expected.
  3. Mark no-shows as "Missed" for follow-up

For recipients who didn't arrive → Mark as Missed:

❌ Mark recipients who didn't show up as "Missed" so they can be followed up with
💡 Tip: Recipients marked as "Missed" can be rescheduled for the next distribution.
